Our Journey

Zahret Misr, a 100% Egyptian company, was founded in 2014 in Cairo, the capital of Egypt. Despite the youthfulness of its founders, they bring more than 10 years of experience. Before entering the world of exporting agricultural products and the international market, the founders underwent training courses in trade, especially in the field of agricultural crops and how to interact with customers and workers.

The company stands out for its effective management, whether in terms of its efficient workforce, dedicated employees, or skilled managers. We operate as one team, where each member contributes to the success of the company. On the customer front, we strive to ensure satisfaction by consistently delivering high-quality Egyptian products. We are committed to achieving the highest levels of customer satisfaction.

Zahret Misr is a truly collaborative partnership, where each member bears a shared responsibility for the success of the company. Our uniqueness lies in positive and professional interactions, making us one of the leading companies in the field of exporting agricultural products. We always look forward to serving our customers efficiently and with quality.

Mission

At Zahret Misr, our mission is to deliver the finest quality agricultural products from Egypt to the world. We aim to achieve this through sustainable farming practices, effective management, and a commitment to customer satisfaction. We believe in building lasting relationships with our clients by consistently providing top-notch products that meet international standards.

Vision

Our vision is to become a global leader in the export of Egyptian agricultural products. We aspire to set the benchmark for quality and reliability in the international market. By leveraging innovation, expertise, and a deep understanding of our customers' needs, we strive to expand our reach and make Egyptian produce a staple in homes and businesses worldwide.

We are committed to promoting sustainable farming practices that benefit both people and the planet Zahret Misr Executives
